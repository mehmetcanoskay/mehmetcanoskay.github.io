Our VALUES

Representing the sequence of beliefs that make our company unique and the cultural characteristics that define the character of our company are as follows; We are Customer Oriented, Reliable, Innovative, Responsible, and Dedicated.

Founded in 2003 as Olgun Medikal San. ve Tic. Ltd. Sti., our company began serving the medical sector for the purpose of radiology consumables sales. Our company also continues to sell other medical equipment, hospital hardware, and various medical supplies besides radiology consumables which it specializes in, adopted the basic principle of honesty in service, sustainability, protecting customer interests, and respecting human rights. Our company, with its organized personnel in the fields of purchasing, marketing, post-sales services, and logistics support, provides a background in the fields of gloves and consumables for the improvement and perfection of the expanding service expectations of public and private health institutions, food and packaging industry and considers a fundamental duty to operate as an institution that made a name in the health, food and packaging sector for years in order to provide quality products to its customers.
